Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, please call our support line at +44 20 7123 4567.

What information should I include in my message?

Please include your full name, contact information, and a detailed description of your inquiry. If you're asking about test results or bookings, please include relevant reference numbers.

Can I book appointments through this form?

While you can request appointments through this form, we recommend using our online booking system for faster scheduling. You can also call us directly to book appointments.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, all communications are encrypted and we follow strict GDPR and healthcare data protection standards. Your personal information is never shared with third parties.
